This project is distributed under the creative commons license and is intended as a DIY home improvement project. The hope is that it will be improved, forked and enhanced by the public for the greater good of the public and the planet. 

You are free to distribute it, save money with it, build it, make a small business out of producing the frames. 

This project corrosponds with the following link

https://savegreen.energy/window-improvements/

Enjoy and please leave feedback and improvements on the design!

The idea is to make the lowest cost upgrades to windows in order to save $300 to $2500 a year in homes for a price that is under $20 per window to make or a distrbuted cost of $50 per window.
